Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common roofing questions

What can I expect once I submit the FREE ESTIMATE form?

One of our experienced professionals will contact you within 2 hours to set an appointment to come to your home.

Do I need to be home when you come look at my roof?

No, you do not have to be at home but it is a good idea to be there so we can explain what was found during the inspection.

How long does it take to get the results of the inspection and Free Estimate?

Our professionals will discuss the results and the necessary Scope of Work details during their initial visit. You will learn the expected needs to correct the issues and the estimated cost.

What type of roofing products do you offer?

We offer multiple Asphalt Shingles options to beautify your home, fit your budget and protect your roof. We also offer tile roofing and flat roofing options as well. Our professional will work with you to ensure the best material and color fit for your home.

How long will it take to replace my roof?

Roof replacement time depends on the size, and sometimes the shape of the roof. An average roof replacement time is 3 days. Our professional will help answer this question once they have completed the FREE ESTIMATE process.

What certifications do you carry?

Burbank Roofing is California State Licensed, a member of the National Roofing Contractor Association, an Accredited Member of the Better Business Bureau with an A+ rating.

Do you offer satellite dish replacement?

No, we do not offer satellite dish replacement. If you have any questions or concerns, please reach out to us and we'll be happy to help.

Does my new roof come with a warranty?

Yes, your roof comes with a 5 year Workmanship Warranty and a Manufacturer's Material Warranty. Our expert professionals will walk you through all the details of the warranty before we perform the work.

What are my payment options?

We accept cash, check, and credit cards and offer financing with approved credit through Wells Fargo. A 3% processing fee applies for credit card transactions.
