Roof Repair

Fast, Reliable Roof Repairs You Can Trust

Professional Roof Repair Services

When your roof is leaking or damaged, you need a fast response from experienced professionals. Burbank Roofing provides prompt, reliable roof repair services to protect your home and prevent further damage. We diagnose the problem correctly the first time and provide lasting solutions.

Common Roof Problems We Repair

  • Roof Leaks - We locate and repair leaks, no matter how elusive. Water stains, mold, or drips are signs you need help now.
  • Missing or Damaged Shingles - Wind, age, and impact can dislodge or crack shingles, leaving your roof vulnerable.
  • Flashing Failures - Damaged or deteriorated flashing around chimneys, vents, and skylights is a leading cause of leaks.
  • Storm Damage - Wind, hail, and fallen branches can cause significant damage requiring immediate attention.
  • Cracked or Broken Tiles - Individual tile repair to maintain your roof's integrity and appearance.
  • Flat Roof Repairs - Membrane damage, ponding water, and seam failures on commercial and residential flat roofs.
  • Gutter-Related Damage - Problems caused by clogged or failing gutters, including fascia rot.
  • Ventilation Issues - Improper ventilation causing moisture buildup, ice dams, or premature roof aging.

Our Roof Repair Process

  1. Inspection - We thoroughly inspect your roof to identify all problems, not just the obvious ones.
  2. Diagnosis - We explain what we found and what's causing the issue.
  3. Estimate - You receive a clear, written estimate before any work begins.
  4. Repair - Our experienced crews complete repairs using quality materials.
  5. Verification - We verify the repair is complete and your roof is watertight.
  6. Warranty - All repairs are backed by our workmanship warranty.

Emergency Roof Repairs

Some situations can't wait. We provide emergency response for:

  • Active leaks during rainstorms
  • Storm damage with exposed roof deck
  • Fallen trees or large branches on roof
  • Fire damage
  • Any situation where delay will cause significant additional damage

For emergencies, we provide temporary tarping and protection until permanent repairs can be completed.

Signs You Need Roof Repair

Don't ignore these warning signs:

  • Water stains on ceilings or walls
  • Dripping water during or after rain
  • Missing, cracked, or curling shingles
  • Granules accumulating in gutters
  • Daylight visible through roof boards (in attic)
  • Sagging areas on the roof
  • Increased energy bills (poor insulation/ventilation)
  • Moss or algae growth
  • Flashing that's rusted or pulling away

Repair vs. Replacement

We'll give you an honest assessment. Sometimes repairs make sense; other times, replacement is more cost-effective. Factors we consider:

  • Roof Age - Repairs on an old roof may be throwing good money after bad.
  • Extent of Damage - Widespread damage may warrant replacement.
  • Number of Repairs - Multiple past repairs suggest systemic issues.
  • Future Plans - If you're selling soon, a new roof may be a better investment.
