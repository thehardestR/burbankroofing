Skylights

Bring Natural Light Into Your Home

Professional Skylight Installation & Repair

Skylights transform dark spaces into bright, welcoming areas filled with natural light. Whether you're looking to add a skylight during a roof replacement or fix a leaky existing skylight, Burbank Roofing provides expert skylight services that ensure proper installation and lasting weather protection.

Our Skylight Services

  • New Skylight Installation - Add natural light to kitchens, bathrooms, hallways, and living spaces.
  • Skylight Replacement - Upgrade old, foggy, or damaged skylights with modern, energy-efficient units.
  • Skylight Leak Repair - Fix leaks caused by improper flashing, seal failures, or damaged glazing.
  • Flashing Replacement - Replace deteriorated flashing around existing skylights.
  • Tubular Skylight Installation - Bring light to interior rooms through small-diameter light tubes.

Types of Skylights We Install

  • Fixed Skylights - Non-opening units designed purely for light. Most economical option.
  • Venting Skylights - Open to allow fresh air circulation. Manual or electric operation available.
  • Solar-Powered Skylights - Electric venting skylights powered by built-in solar panels. No wiring required.
  • Tubular Skylights (Sun Tunnels) - Compact tubes that channel light from roof to ceiling. Ideal for small spaces and interior rooms.
  • Custom Skylights - Large or uniquely shaped skylights for architectural applications.

Benefits of Skylights

  • Natural Light - Skylights provide up to 3x more light than vertical windows of the same size.
  • Energy Savings - Reduce need for artificial lighting during the day.
  • Ventilation - Venting skylights help remove hot air and improve air circulation.
  • Mood & Health - Natural light improves mood, productivity, and overall well-being.
  • Home Value - Skylights are a desirable feature that can increase property value.
  • Privacy - Get natural light in spaces where windows would compromise privacy.

Why Skylights Leak (And How We Prevent It)

Improperly installed skylights are notorious for leaking. Common causes include:

  • Improper Flashing - We use step flashing and continuous flashing systems designed for your roof type.
  • Poor Curb Construction - We build proper curbs that shed water away from the skylight.
  • Seal Failures - We use high-quality sealants and proper installation techniques.
  • Condensation - We ensure proper insulation around the light shaft to prevent condensation issues.

Skylight Features to Consider

  • Glazing Options - Clear, tinted, or frosted glass; tempered or laminated for safety.
  • Low-E Coatings - Reduce heat gain and UV damage to furnishings.
  • Blinds & Shades - Manual, electric, or solar-powered options for light control.
  • Rain Sensors - Automatically close venting skylights when rain is detected.
  • Remote Control - Operate skylights from anywhere in the room.

Best Locations for Skylights

  • Kitchens - Brighten work areas and reduce daytime lighting costs
  • Bathrooms - Natural light and ventilation where privacy matters
  • Hallways - Illuminate dark corridors
  • Stairwells - Add safety and ambiance
  • Home offices - Improve productivity with natural light
  • Living rooms - Create dramatic lighting effects
