Metal Roofing

Durable, Long-Lasting Metal Roof Systems

Premium Metal Roofing Installation

Metal roofing has become one of the most popular choices for homeowners and businesses seeking durability, energy efficiency, and long-term value. At Burbank Roofing, we specialize in professional metal roof installation that protects your property for decades.

Types of Metal Roofing We Install

  • Standing Seam Metal Roofing - The premium choice with concealed fasteners for a sleek, modern look and superior weather protection.
  • Metal Shingles - The appearance of traditional shingles with the durability of metal. Available in styles that mimic slate, tile, or wood shake.
  • Corrugated Metal Panels - Cost-effective option ideal for agricultural, industrial, and modern residential applications.
  • Stone-Coated Steel - Combines the strength of steel with the beauty of traditional roofing materials.
  • Aluminum Roofing - Lightweight, corrosion-resistant, ideal for coastal properties.
  • Copper Roofing - Premium option that develops a beautiful patina over time.

Benefits of Metal Roofing

  • Exceptional Longevity - Metal roofs last 40-70 years, compared to 15-20 years for asphalt shingles.
  • Energy Efficiency - Reflective coatings can reduce cooling costs by 10-25% in hot climates like Southern California.
  • Durability - Withstands winds up to 140 mph, won't crack, and is impact-resistant.
  • Fire Resistant - Class A fire rating provides maximum fire protection.
  • Low Maintenance - Requires minimal upkeep compared to other roofing materials.
  • Environmentally Friendly - Made from 25-95% recycled content and 100% recyclable at end of life.
  • Increased Home Value - Metal roofs can increase property value and appeal to buyers.
  • Lightweight - Weighs less than tile or slate, often can be installed over existing roofing.

Metal Roofing Colors & Finishes

Today's metal roofing comes in a wide variety of colors and finishes to complement any architectural style:

  • Classic colors: Black, Charcoal, Bronze, Gray
  • Earth tones: Forest Green, Terra Cotta, Copper Brown
  • Cool colors: Colonial Red, Ocean Blue, Slate Blue
  • Premium finishes: Kynar 500®/Hylar 5000® for maximum fade resistance

Is Metal Roofing Right for You?

Metal roofing is an excellent choice if you:

  • Plan to stay in your home long-term
  • Want to reduce energy costs
  • Live in an area prone to wildfires
  • Want a low-maintenance roofing solution
  • Are concerned about environmental impact
  • Want to increase your home's resale value

Common Metal Roofing Questions

Is metal roofing noisy? No! When properly installed with solid sheathing and underlayment, metal roofs are no louder than other roofing types during rain.

Will a metal roof attract lightning? No. Metal roofs don't attract lightning, and if struck, they safely disperse the energy. Plus, they won't catch fire.

Can I walk on a metal roof? Yes, with proper care. We'll show you the safe areas to walk for maintenance access.
