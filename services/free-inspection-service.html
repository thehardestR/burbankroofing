Free Roof Inspection

Know Your Roof's True Condition

Comprehensive Free Roof Inspections

Your roof protects everything you value. Don't wait for a leak to find out there's a problem. Burbank Roofing offers free, no-obligation roof inspections that give you a clear picture of your roof's condition and help you plan for the future.

What Our Free Inspection Includes

  • Exterior Roof Inspection - We examine all roofing materials, looking for wear, damage, and potential problem areas.
  • Flashing Inspection - We check all flashing around chimneys, vents, skylights, and edges.
  • Gutter Assessment - We inspect gutters and downspouts for proper function and condition.
  • Ventilation Check - We verify your roof has adequate ventilation to prevent moisture and heat problems.
  • Attic Inspection - When accessible, we look for signs of leaks, moisture, or inadequate insulation.
  • Photo Documentation - We take photos of any issues we find so you can see what we see.
  • Written Report - You receive a detailed assessment of your roof's condition.
  • Honest Recommendations - We'll tell you if repairs are needed, if your roof is fine, or if replacement should be planned.

When to Schedule a Roof Inspection

  • After Severe Weather - High winds, hail, and heavy rain can cause hidden damage.
  • Before Rainy Season - Identify and fix problems before they become leaks.
  • When Buying or Selling - Know the roof's condition before closing the deal.
  • If Your Roof is 10+ Years Old - Regular inspections catch problems early.
  • If You Notice Warning Signs - Stains, missing shingles, or granules in gutters.
  • After Tree Work - Falling branches can cause damage you can't see from the ground.
  • Annually - Preventive inspections save money in the long run.

Why Free? What's the Catch?

There's no catch. We offer free inspections because:

  • It lets us build a relationship with you based on trust and transparency.
  • An informed homeowner is our best customer.
  • If you do need work, you'll already know and trust us.
  • We believe in earning your business, not pressuring you into it.

There's no obligation. If your roof is in good shape, we'll tell you. We won't invent problems to sell you services you don't need.

What Happens After the Inspection?

  • We review our findings with you on-site or via phone.
  • You receive a written report with photos.
  • If repairs or replacement are needed, we provide a detailed estimate.
  • You decide if and when to proceed - no pressure.
  • We're happy to answer questions anytime.

How to Prepare for Your Inspection

  • Clear access to attic if you want interior inspection
  • Secure or bring pets inside
  • Note any specific concerns you want us to address
  • Have any previous inspection or repair records available
