Protect Your Home from Water Damage

Full Gutter Replacement Services

Your gutter system is essential for directing rainwater away from your home's foundation, siding, and landscaping. When gutters fail, water damage can cost thousands in repairs. Burbank Roofing provides complete gutter replacement services to protect your home investment.

Our Gutter Services

  • Complete Gutter Replacement - Remove old gutters and install new, properly sized gutter systems.
  • Seamless Gutter Installation - Custom-fabricated on-site to fit your home perfectly with no leak-prone seams.
  • Downspout Installation - Properly sized and positioned downspouts for efficient drainage.
  • Gutter Guard Installation - Keep leaves and debris out to reduce maintenance and prevent clogs.
  • Fascia Board Repair - Replace rotted fascia boards before gutter installation.
  • Gutter Repairs - Fix leaks, resecure loose sections, and replace damaged areas.

Types of Gutters We Install

  • Seamless Aluminum - The most popular choice. Lightweight, rust-resistant, and available in many colors.
  • Copper Gutters - Premium option that develops a beautiful patina. Lasts 50+ years.
  • Galvanized Steel - Strong and durable, ideal for areas with heavy debris.
  • K-Style Gutters - The standard profile that handles high water volume.
  • Half-Round Gutters - Traditional style perfect for historic or Spanish-style homes.
  • Box Gutters - Built into the roof structure for a hidden appearance.

Why Gutters Matter

Properly functioning gutters prevent:

  • Foundation Damage - Water pooling around foundations causes cracks, settling, and basement flooding.
  • Siding Damage - Overflowing gutters allow water to run down walls, causing rot and mold.
  • Landscape Erosion - Uncontrolled runoff washes away soil and damages plants.
  • Fascia and Soffit Rot - Standing water in clogged gutters rots the wood behind them.
  • Ice Dams - In colder climates, clogged gutters contribute to damaging ice dams.
  • Pest Infestations - Standing water attracts mosquitoes; debris attracts nesting pests.

Signs You Need New Gutters

  • Visible cracks, holes, or rust spots
  • Gutters pulling away from the house
  • Sagging or uneven sections
  • Peeling paint or rust on gutters
  • Water pooling around foundation after rain
  • Water stains or mildew on siding
  • Gutters overflow during rain despite cleaning
  • Multiple seam failures or leaks

Gutter Guard Options

We offer several gutter protection solutions:

  • Mesh Guards - Fine mesh keeps out leaves, pine needles, and debris.
  • Reverse Curve Guards - Water follows the curve into the gutter; debris falls off.
  • Brush Guards - Bristles fill the gutter, allowing water through while blocking debris.
  • Foam Inserts - Porous foam fills the gutter, letting water through while keeping debris out.

Proper Gutter Sizing

One of the most common gutter problems is undersized gutters. We calculate the proper gutter size based on:

  • Roof square footage
  • Roof pitch (steep roofs shed water faster)
  • Local rainfall intensity
  • Number and placement of downspouts
