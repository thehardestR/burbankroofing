Tile Roofing

Beautiful, Timeless Tile Roofs for California Homes

Classic Tile Roofing Installation

Tile roofing is the quintessential California roofing style. For over a century, tile roofs have graced homes throughout Southern California, providing unmatched beauty, durability, and protection. At Burbank Roofing, we specialize in tile roof installation, repair, and replacement.

Types of Tile Roofing We Install

  • Clay Tiles - Traditional terracotta tiles that have been used for centuries. Natural clay provides excellent durability and a classic aesthetic.
  • Concrete Tiles - More affordable than clay with similar benefits. Available in many styles and colors.
  • Spanish "S" Tiles - The iconic barrel-shaped tile that defines Mediterranean and Spanish Colonial architecture.
  • Flat Tiles - Clean, modern look while maintaining tile benefits. Popular for contemporary homes.
  • Interlocking Tiles - Engineered for superior wind resistance and easier installation.
  • Slate-Look Tiles - Concrete tiles designed to mimic the appearance of natural slate.

Benefits of Tile Roofing

  • Exceptional Longevity - Tile roofs can last 50-100+ years with proper maintenance.
  • Fire Resistant - Class A fire rating, crucial for California's fire-prone regions.
  • Energy Efficient - Natural thermal resistance keeps homes cooler in summer.
  • Weather Resistant - Handles heavy rain, high winds, and intense sun.
  • Low Maintenance - Individual tiles can be replaced without disturbing the entire roof.
  • Increased Home Value - Tile roofs are highly desirable and add curb appeal.
  • Sustainable - Made from natural materials, recyclable, and long-lasting.
  • Sound Insulation - Provides excellent noise reduction from rain and outside sounds.

Tile Roofing Colors & Styles

Tile roofing offers endless design possibilities:

  • Terra Cotta - Classic orange-red that develops character over time
  • Earth Tones - Browns, tans, and warm neutrals
  • Cool Grays - Modern slate and charcoal options
  • Multi-Color Blends - Variegated tiles for visual interest
  • Custom Colors - Many manufacturers offer custom color matching

Our Tile Roofing Services

  • New Tile Roof Installation - Complete installation including underlayment, flashing, and ventilation.
  • Tile Roof Replacement - Remove old roofing and install new tile system.
  • Tile Roof Repair - Replace broken or cracked tiles, fix leaks, and restore flashing.
  • Tile Roof Maintenance - Regular inspections, cleaning, and preventive care.
  • Underlayment Replacement - Replace worn underlayment while preserving existing tiles.

Important Considerations for Tile Roofs

Structural Requirements: Tile roofs are heavier than asphalt shingles. We'll assess your roof structure to ensure it can support the weight, and reinforce if necessary.

Professional Installation Required: Tile roofing requires specialized skills. Improper installation can lead to leaks and premature tile failure.

Walking on Tile: Tile can crack if walked on improperly. Our crews are trained in proper tile roof access techniques.
