Expert Commercial Roofing Services

Your commercial property requires a roofing system that can withstand heavy use, protect valuable inventory and equipment, and minimize business disruption. Burbank Roofing delivers professional commercial roofing solutions designed for durability and performance.

Our Commercial Roofing Services Include:

  • New Construction Roofing - Complete roofing systems for new commercial buildings, warehouses, and retail spaces.
  • Roof Replacement - When your commercial roof has reached the end of its lifespan, we provide efficient tear-off and replacement.
  • Roof Repairs - Quick response to leaks and damage to minimize disruption to your business operations.
  • Preventive Maintenance Programs - Regular inspections and maintenance to extend roof life and prevent costly repairs.
  • Emergency Services - 24/7 emergency response for storm damage and critical leaks.
  • Roof Coatings - Extend the life of your existing roof with reflective coatings that improve energy efficiency.

Commercial Roofing Systems We Install

  • TPO (Thermoplastic Polyolefin) - Energy-efficient, durable, and cost-effective for flat roofs.
  • EPDM Rubber Roofing - Proven performance and excellent weather resistance.
  • Modified Bitumen - Multi-layer protection ideal for flat and low-slope roofs.
  • Built-Up Roofing (BUR) - Traditional tar and gravel systems for superior protection.
  • Metal Roofing - Long-lasting and low-maintenance for industrial applications.
  • SPF Foam Roofing - Seamless, energy-efficient spray foam systems.
  • PVC Roofing - Chemical-resistant option for restaurants and industrial facilities.

Signs Your Commercial Roof Needs Attention

  • Ponding water that doesn't drain within 48 hours
  • Visible cracks, blisters, or membrane damage
  • Interior water stains or active leaks
  • Rising energy costs (poor insulation)
  • Flashing separation at edges and penetrations
  • Roof age exceeding 15-20 years
